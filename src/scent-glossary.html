Scent Glossary

Fragrance is a language. Use this glossary to understand the notes and families that make up our signature scents, helping you find your perfect match.

Fruity & Sweet

Bright, playful, and uplifting. These scents are built around the delicious aromas of ripe fruits and sweet notes like vanilla or caramel.

Find it in: Aloha Luxe, Strawberry Rosé

Earthy & Woody

Warm, grounding, and sophisticated. These fragrances feature rich notes like sandalwood, cedar, patchouli, and vetiver, often balanced with spices or herbs.

Find it in: Autumn Velvet, Bleu Crème Luxe

Fresh & Clean

Crisp, airy, and invigorating. Think of fresh linens, ocean breezes, and citrus zests. These scents are perfect for creating a clean, light, and revitalizing atmosphere.

Find it in: Coastal Oasis, Southern Charm

Floral & Romantic

Elegant, soft, and classic. From delicate jasmine to bold rose, these scents capture the timeless beauty of a blooming garden. They can be soft and powdery or lush and complex.

Find it in: Enchanted Kiss
