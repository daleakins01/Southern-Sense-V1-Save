Candle Care 101

Get the most out of your Southern Sense candle with these simple tips.

1

The First Burn is Key

When you light your candle for the first time, allow it to burn until the melted wax pool reaches the entire edge of the jar. This can take 2-4 hours. This prevents "tunneling" and ensures an even burn for the life of your candle.

2

Trim Your Wick

Before every burn, trim your wick to about 1/4 inch. This prevents a large, "mushroomed" wick, which can cause excess soot and smoke. You can use a wick trimmer, nail clippers, or even your fingers to pinch off the charred top.

3

Watch the Time

Never burn your candle for more than 4 hours at a time. This prevents the jar from overheating and ensures the wick doesn't become unstable. Always burn on a flat, heat-resistant surface, away from drafts, children, and pets.

4

Know When to Say Goodbye

Discontinue use when only about 1/2 inch of wax remains at the bottom. Burning it further can be a fire hazard as the flame can get too close to the bottom of the jar. It's time to pick out your next scent!
