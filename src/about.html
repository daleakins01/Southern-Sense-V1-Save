Our Commitment to Quality

The Finest Wax

We use a premium, all-natural soy wax blend. This ensures a clean, slow burn that's eco-friendly and free from harmful toxins, giving you a purer fragrance experience.

Your health matters. Our scents are crafted from the highest quality, phthalate-free fragrance oils, so you can breathe easy knowing your air is clean.

Every single one of our products is hand-poured with meticulous care in small batches right here in North Carolina. This ensures unmatched quality and attention to detail.
