The Secret Boss Page Guide (Admin Panel)

This is the control room for our whole store! You can check orders, make more candles, and change the words on the website. Use the buttons on the left to jump to a section.

1. Dashboard (The Store's Report Card)

What it does: This is like a quick morning check-up. It shows if our store is healthy and what we need to work on right now.

  • **Total Orders:** Tells us how many things people bought.
  • **Pending Reviews:** Shows how many people wrote little notes (reviews) that you need to read before they go on the website.
  • **Active Stock Alerts:** Tells us how many people are waiting for a sold-out candle to come back in stock.
  • **Low Stock Items (WARNING!):** Shows the list of products we are almost out of (10 or less left). Time to make more!

2. Orders Tab (The Mailbox)

What it does: This is where you see everything people have bought so we know what to put in the mail. The newest orders are always at the top.

  • **Status:** Tells you if the order is **Paid** (good to ship) or **Pending** (waiting for money).
  • **Action: Details:** Click this to see the person's name and address so you know where to send the candle boxes.

3. Products Tab (The Warehouse)

What it does: This lists every single candle and wax melt we sell. This is where you count the inventory!

  • **Stock:** Shows exactly how many we have right now. If this is 0, the product is sold out.
  • **Featured:** If this says "Featured," it means the candle is special and is on the front page of the website.
  • **Action: Edit:** Click to open a little window where you can change the **Price** or update the **Stock** number after you make a new batch of candles.

4. Reviews Tab (The Note Checker)

What it does: People write notes (reviews) to tell us if they liked the candle. You have to check them before they go live!

  • **Action: Approve:** Click this button when you read a nice note! It makes the note show up on the website for everyone to see.
  • **Action: Unapprove:** Click this if a note is mean or confusing. It hides the note from the public.
  • **Action: Delete Permanently:** Click this to throw the note away forever (only use this if you have to).

5. Stock Alerts Tab (The Waiting List)

What it does: This is the list of customers who are waiting for a sold-out candle to come back.

  • **Action: Email Customer:** Click this link to send the customer an email to tell them, "Good news! Your candle is back!"
  • **Action: Mark as Resolved:** **Click this button AFTER you have sent the email.** It takes the person off the waiting list so you don't get confused.

6. Site Content Tab (The Word Changer)

What it does: This is a magical button! It lets you change the words and pictures on the public pages, like the **About Us** story or the **Candle Care** tips. No coding needed!

  • **Managed Pages:** You can change almost everything: the Homepage, About Us, Candle Care, Refill Program, Gifting, Subscriptions, and our Scent Guides.
  • **Action:** Click the page you want to edit, change the words in the boxes, and click **"Save Page Content"**.

7. Tools / Utility (The Secret Power Box)

What it does: This tab has secret, strong buttons. These are for when the big boss needs to make a copy of ALL the store's information or load a backup copy.

  • **Rule:** **DO NOT** click or touch anything in this section unless an adult developer tells you it's okay. It can erase all our candles!
