How It Works

We believe in sustainability and rewarding our loyal customers. Our refill program is simple: bring back your empty Southern Sense candle jars and get a discount on your next purchase.

The Details

  • Available for in-person returns only at our participating markets and events.
  • Jars must be clean and in good condition (no cracks or major chips).
  • Receive a discount (typically $1 off per jar) to be applied immediately to your new purchase.

Why We Do It

Every jar reused is one less item in a landfill. It's our small way of saying thank you to you and to our planet. Together, we can make a difference, one candle at a time.
