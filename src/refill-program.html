Southern Sense Refill Program

Welcome to our sustainable refill program! We're thrilled to offer a way to enjoy your favorite Southern Sense candles while being kind to the planet (and your wallet).

How It Works

Our program is simple. You bring us your *clean* and *undamaged* Southern Sense candle jars, and we'll refill them with the scent of your choice for a discounted price.

Refill Pricing

Refills are priced based on the size of the jar. This offers you a significant savings compared to purchasing a new candle each time.

  • Signature Jars (approx. 8-10 oz): $15.00
  • Luxe Collection Jars (approx. 12-14 oz): $22.00
  • Wax Melts (requires your own container): $7.00 per 3 oz.

Please note: Prices are subject to change. This program is currently available for local drop-off and pick-up only.

Guidelines for Refills

To ensure a high-quality and safe refill, we have a few important guidelines:

  1. Only Southern Sense Jars: We can only accept jars that were originally purchased from Southern Sense. We cannot verify the safety and heat resistance of other containers.
  2. Clean Jars Only: Jars must be returned completely clean. This means all old wax, wick-tabs, and residue must be removed. Jars with residue will be returned to you un-filled.
  3. No Damaged Jars: We cannot refill any jars that have chips, cracks, or deep scratches. This is a critical safety measure, as damaged glass can break when heated.
  4. Wick & Scent Choice: You can choose any scent from our current collection for your refill, even if it's different from the original!

How to Clean Your Jar

The easiest way to clean your jar is to place it in the freezer for a few hours. The cold will cause the wax to shrink and pop out easily. You can then use a spoon to (gently!) remove the metal wick tab at the bottom.

After the wax is out, wash the jar thoroughly with hot, soapy water to remove any remaining oil or residue. Dry it completely.
