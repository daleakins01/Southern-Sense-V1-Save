Southern Sense Admin Panel User Guide

This panel is the command center, giving you full control over all e-commerce operations, product catalog, and public-facing content. All changes are saved instantly to the database.

1. Dashboard (The Control Center)

Purpose: Provides a real-time, high-level summary of store health and pending operational tasks.

  • **Total Orders:** The aggregate count of all completed and pending customer orders.
  • **Pending Reviews:** The number of customer reviews currently awaiting moderation and approval.
  • **Active Stock Alerts:** The total count of customers waiting for an item to be restocked.
  • **Low Stock Items (CRITICAL):** The number of unique products where the inventory level is **10 or less**. These items require immediate attention.

2. Orders Tab (Order Management)

Purpose: Track all sales and monitor shipping status.

  • **Data Source:** Reads all documents from the /orders collection.
  • **Functionality:** Lists orders, sorted newest first. Displays Order # (truncated), Date, Total, Status (Paid, Shipped, Pending, etc.), and Customer.
  • **Action:** The "Details" button provides a popup with full customer shipping information and itemized line items for fulfillment.

3. Products Tab (Catalog & Inventory)

Purpose: Manage inventory, pricing, and product visibility.

  • **Data Source:** Reads and writes to the /products collection.
  • **Functionality:** Lists all products with their Scent Family, Price, Stock Level, and **Featured** status.
  • **Action:** The "Edit" button opens a modal allowing instantaneous updates to core fields, including `stock` and the critical `featured: boolean` flag (used to promote a product on the Homepage).

4. Reviews Tab (Social Proof Moderation)

Purpose: Moderate and approve customer feedback before it appears on product pages.

  • **Data Source:** Reads and writes to the /reviews collection.
  • **Functionality:** Fetches all reviews, prioritizing those with approved: false (Pending).
  • **Actions:**
    • **Approve:** Sets `approved: true`. Review immediately appears on the corresponding product page.
    • **Unapprove:** Sets `approved: false`. Hides the review.
    • **Delete Permanently:** Removes the review from the database entirely.

5. Stock Alerts Tab (Acquisition Management)

Purpose: View and clear back-in-stock requests from customers.

  • **Data Source:** Reads and deletes from the /backInStockRequests collection.
  • **Functionality:** Lists all unresolved requests, indicating the customer email and requested product name.
  • **Actions:**
    • **Email Customer:** Provides a quick `mailto:` link to notify the customer of restock.
    • **Mark as Resolved:** **Deletes the request from the database**, clearing the alert from the Admin queue (used after stock has been replenished and the customer has been notified).

6. Site Content Tab (CMS)

Purpose: Update the text and content blocks on all major public pages without touching code.

  • **Data Source:** Reads and writes to the /siteContent collection.
  • **Managed Documents:** You can manage: homepage, about, candleCare, refillProgram, gifting, subscriptions, scent-guide, and scent-glossary.
  • **Functionality:** Dynamically loads all text fields (including rich HTML fields) for the selected page and allows you to save changes instantly.

7. Tools / Utility (Developer Access)

Purpose: Provides developer utilities for manual database maintenance, typically for backup or restoration.

  • **Export Database:** Provides the command (`node export-firestore.mjs`) to create a local, safe JSON backup of the entire Firestore database.
  • **Import Database:** Provides the command (`node import-firestore.mjs`) to restore the database from a local JSON file. **Warning:** This will overwrite your current live data.
