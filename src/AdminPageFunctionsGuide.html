The Secret Boss Page Guide

This is the biggest control room for our whole store! You can do everything here.

1. Dashboard (The Store's Report Card)

What it does: This is a quick look to see if the store is happy and healthy. Red numbers mean we need to work fast!

  • **Total Orders:** How many things people bought *forever*.
  • **Total Revenue:** How much money the store made *forever*.
  • **MTD Revenue (New!):** How much money the store made just in the last month.
  • **Active Users (New!):** How many people looked at the website recently.
  • **Avg. Order Value:** How much money people spend on average when they buy things.
  • **Pending Reviews:** Notes from customers you need to read and approve.
  • **Low Stock Items:** Candles we are almost out of (hurry, make more!).
  • **Out of Stock Items:** Candles we have ZERO of (make these first!).
  • **New Inquiries:** New notes from customers that need an answer.

2. Orders Tab (The Mailbox)

What it does: This shows what people bought, so we know what to ship.

  • **Status:** Tells you if the money is **Paid** (good!) or **Pending** (waiting).
  • **Action: Details:** Click this to see the person's name and address to send their box.

3. Products Tab (The Warehouse - FULL POWER!)

What it does: This is the list of ALL candles. You can change everything here!

  • **Action: Edit:** Click to open a little window to change the **Price** or the **Stock** number.
  • **Action: Delete:** Click this red button to throw a candle out of the store *forever*.
  • **+ Add New Product:** Click the big button at the top to put a **brand new candle** in the store.

4. Reviews Tab (The Note Checker)

What it does: Customers send notes about the candles. You decide if they go on the website.

  • **Action: Approve:** Click to make the nice note show up on the website.
  • **Action: Unapprove:** Click to hide a confusing or mean note.

5. Stock Alerts Tab (The Waiting List)

What it does: People who want a sold-out candle wait here.

  • **Action: Email Customer:** Click to tell them, "Good news! Your candle is back!"
  • **Action: Mark as Resolved:** Click this *after* you tell them, so they leave the waiting list.

6. Customer Inquiries Tab (The Customer Mailbox)

What it does: This is the special mailbox for notes people send from the "Contact Us" page. It holds all their messages.

  • **Action: Reply to Email:** Click to send a friendly note back to the customer.
  • **Action: Mark as Resolved (Delete):** Click *after* you answer the note, so your mailbox stays clean.

7. Site Content Tab (The Word & Picture Changer)

What it does: This is a magical button! It lets you change the words on almost every page! You can edit:

  • The Homepage big welcome words.
  • The About Us story and the Candle Care tips.
  • The secret email address where contact messages are sent.
  • **Action:** Click the page, change the words, and click **"Save Page Content"**.

8. Tools / Utility (The Secret Power Box - EVERYTHING POSSIBLE!)

What it does: This tab has strong buttons to make the website fast and safe. It has ALL the tools!

  • **PURGE ALL:** This button tells the website's memory (the "cache") to forget everything, so the whole world sees your new changes instantly and the store runs super fast.
  • **BUILD NOW:** This button tells the robot to build a fresh, new copy of the store right away.
  • **Backup/Restore:** These buttons are for the big boss only! **DO NOT** click or touch these. They can erase all our candles!
