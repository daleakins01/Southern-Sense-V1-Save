Admin Dashboard Guide

Welcome to your Southern Sense Admin Panel! This guide explains the key functions available to manage your online store.

1. Logging In & Out

  • Use the Admin Login Page with your specific admin email and password.
  • Click the "Sign Out" button in the top-right corner of the Admin Panel header to securely log out.

2. Navigation Tabs

Use the tabs at the top to switch between different management sections:

  • Dashboard: An overview of key store metrics.
  • Products: Manage your product listings.
  • Orders: View customer orders and update their status.
  • Reviews: Moderate incoming customer reviews.
  • Site Content: Edit text content on various website pages.

3. Dashboard Tab

Provides a quick snapshot of your store's performance:

  • Total Revenue: The sum of all completed order totals.
  • Total Orders: The total number of orders received.
  • Avg. Order Value: Total Revenue divided by Total Orders.
  • Total Products: The total number of products listed in your store.
  • Out of Stock: The number of products currently with a stock level of 0.
  • Pending Reviews: The number of customer reviews awaiting your approval.
  • Approved Reviews: The total number of reviews currently visible on the site.

4. Products Tab

This is where you manage your product inventory.

  • "Add New Product" Button: Opens a form (modal window) to add a new item to your store. Fill in all details carefully. The 'Product ID' will be automatically generated from the name when adding a new product.
  • Search Bar: Type a product name or ID to quickly filter the list below.
  • Product Data Tools:
    • "Export All Products (JSON)": Downloads a detailed JSON file of all your product data. Useful for backups or analysis.
    • "Export All (CSV)": Downloads a spreadsheet (CSV) file of all your products.
    • "Choose File" & "Import & Update": Allows you to upload an edited CSV file to update multiple products at once. Make sure the CSV follows the same format as the exported file, especially the 'id' column.
  • Product Cards: Displays each product's image, name, price, category, scent family, and current stock.
    • "Update Stock" Button: Quickly change the stock number for a product without opening the full edit form.
    • "Edit" Button: Opens the form pre-filled with that product's details for you to change.
    • "Delete" Button: Permanently removes a product from your store (use with caution!).

5. Orders Tab

View and manage incoming customer orders.

  • Search Bar: Type an Order ID, Customer Name, or Email to quickly filter the list.
  • Order List Table: Shows key details for each order.
    • Status Dropdown: Change the status of an order (e.g., from "New" to "Processing" or "Shipped"). The change is saved automatically.
    • "View" Button: Opens a modal window showing the full order details, including items purchased and shipping address.

6. Reviews Tab

Moderate new reviews submitted by customers.

  • Search Bar: Type a Product Name, Reviewer Name, or text from the review to filter the list.
  • Review Cards: Shows each new review awaiting moderation.
  • "Approve" Button: Makes the review public on the relevant product page.
  • "Delete" Button: Permanently deletes the review.

7. Site Content Tab

Edit text content displayed on specific pages of your website without needing to change the code.

  • Click on a section title (e.g., "Homepage," "About Us Page") to expand it.
  • Edit the text in the input fields or text areas.
  • Click the "Save All Content" button at the bottom to save changes across all sections.
