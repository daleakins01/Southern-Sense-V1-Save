Our Story

From a simple hobby to a cherished business, this is Southern Sense.

Welcome to Southern Sense! My name is Paula Bryan, and I'm the heart and hands behind every candle you see. What started as a creative outlet in my kitchen has blossomed into a passion I’m thrilled to share with you.

Based in the charming town of Kinston, North Carolina, Southern Sense is built on the belief that scent is a powerful part of our lives. It’s tied to our memories, our moods, and our homes. That's why I pour so much care into every small batch.

I hand-pour each candle using a premium soy-paraffin wax blend, which provides the best of both worlds: the clean burn of soy and the exceptional scent-throw of paraffin. Paired with high-quality, phthalate-free fragrance oils and lead-free cotton wicks, the result is a beautifully aromatic, long-lasting candle that you can feel good about burning.

Thank you for supporting my small business. I hope my candles bring as much warmth and joy to your home as I find in making them.

Paula Bryan
